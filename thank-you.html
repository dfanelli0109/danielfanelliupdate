About Daniel Fanelli

I am a Front End/Full Stack Web Developer with previous experience in web site development, data management, and video/audio production. I have an eye for creativity, a skill for analyzing data and a the strong drive to provide excellence. I have a BA Degree in Radio/TV Communications from the University of Central Florida, and am also CompTIA A+, Net+, and HFMA CRCR Certified.

  • Studied digital media and business administration at the University of Central Florida.
    1. 2001: Graduated with a BA Degree in Communications.
    2. Held several internships at AMFM and Cox Media.
    3. Worked as a student employee assisting production of the Coach's show, a UCF production.
  • 1996-2004: Served in various production roles and as technical director for several ministries.
  • 2007-2010: Owned A Majestic Moments Digital Studios, a special event and wedding videography company and maintained company website.
  • 2009-2017: Worked as a Medical Biller and in 2016 became HFMA CRCR Certified.
    • 2014-2015: Worked as a Data Manager at CoopDigity.
  • Attended a 12 Week ASP.NET/C# Full Stack Web Developer Boot Camp at Coder Camps.
